## Brand Name Usage

### Official Brand Name

**Primary:** Jerry Can Spirits®
**Legal Entity:** Jerry Can Spirits® Ltd

### First Use Rule

On first prominent use in any document, webpage, or material, always use the full registered trademark:

✅ **CORRECT:**
"Jerry Can Spirits® is a premium British rum brand..."

After the first prominent use, the ® symbol may be used periodically but is not required on every subsequent mention.

### Capitalization

Always capitalize "Jerry," "Can," and "Spirits" when referring to the brand:

✅ **CORRECT:** Jerry Can Spirits®
❌ **INCORRECT:** jerry can spirits, Jerry can Spirits, JERRY CAN SPIRITS

**Exception:** All caps may be used for emphasis in headlines or promotional materials, but maintain proper spacing:

✅ **ACCEPTABLE:** JERRY CAN SPIRITS® (for headlines only)

### Spacing

The brand name consists of three separate words. Do not:
- Remove spaces: ❌ JerryCanSpirits®
- Use hyphens: ❌ Jerry-Can-Spirits®
- Add extra words: ❌ The Jerry Can Spirits®

✅ **CORRECT:** Jerry Can Spirits®

## Logo Usage

### Primary Logo

The Jerry Can Spirits® figurative logo is a registered trademark. Use the official logo files provided in the `/images/` directory:

- **Header Logo:** `/images/Logo.webp`
- **Footer Logo:** `/images/logo-etch.webp`

### Clear Space

Maintain a minimum clear space around the logo equal to the height of the "J" in "Jerry" on all sides. This ensures the logo remains distinct and uncluttered.

### Minimum Size

**Digital:**
- Minimum width: 120px
- Recommended width: 160-200px for headers

**Print:**
- Minimum width: 1 inch (25mm)

### Logo Backgrounds

**Preferred backgrounds:**
- Dark backgrounds (jerry-green-900, jerry-green-800)
- Parchment/light backgrounds with proper contrast

**Avoid:**
- Busy patterns that obscure the logo
- Low-contrast backgrounds
- Placing over images without proper backdrop

### Logo Modifications

❌ **DO NOT:**
- Change logo colors
- Distort, stretch, or rotate the logo
- Add effects (shadows, glows, outlines)
- Recreate or redraw the logo
- Separate or rearrange logo elements
- Place logo inside shapes or containers

✅ **DO:**
- Use official logo files only
- Maintain aspect ratio when scaling
- Ensure sufficient contrast with background
- Include ® symbol in alt text: `alt="Jerry Can Spirits®"`

## Registered Trademark Symbol (®)

### When to Use ®

Always use the ® symbol in the following contexts:

1. **First prominent use** on every webpage, document, or material
2. **Legal documents** (Terms of Service, Privacy Policy, contracts)
3. **Copyright notices** in footers
4. **Marketing materials** (first use in brochures, ads, emails)
5. **Product packaging** and labels
6. **Press releases** and media materials
7. **Social media profiles** (bio/about sections)
8. **Meta tags and SEO** elements

### Where to Place ®

The ® symbol should appear immediately after "Spirits" as a superscript:

✅ **CORRECT:** Jerry Can Spirits®
❌ **INCORRECT:** Jerry® Can Spirits, Jerry Can® Spirits®

### Implementation Examples

#### HTML/Web
```html
Jerry Can Spirits<sup>®</sup>
<!-- or -->
Jerry Can Spirits&reg;
```

#### React/JSX
```jsx
<span>Jerry Can Spirits®</span>
```

#### Markdown
```markdown
Jerry Can Spirits®
```

#### Plain Text
```
Jerry Can Spirits®
```

### When ® May Be Omitted

After the first prominent use in a document, subsequent mentions may omit the ® symbol, though periodic use is recommended. The symbol may be omitted in:

- Body text after first use (optional)
- Social media posts (after first use)
- Internal communications
- Informal contexts where space is constrained

**Important:** Never use ® for products or services not covered by the registered classes (33 and 35).

## Prohibited Uses

### Trademark Violations

❌ **DO NOT:**

1. **Use the trademark without authorization** for commercial purposes
2. **Modify the registered mark** in any way
3. **Create derivatives** or variations of the brand name
4. **Use similar marks** that could cause confusion
5. **Incorporate our trademark** into your company name, product name, or domain name
6. **Use our trademark** in a misleading or disparaging manner
7. **Imply endorsement** or partnership without written agreement
8. **Use ®** with unregistered variations of the mark

### Brand Misuse

❌ **Incorrect Usage Examples:**

- "Jerry-Can-Spirits" (hyphens)
- "JerryCanSpirits" (no spaces)
- "The Jerry Can Spirits" (adding articles)
- "Jerry Can Spirit" (singular)
- "JCS" (unauthorized abbreviation without context)
- "Jerry® Can Spirits®" (multiple ® symbols)

### Logo Misuse

❌ **DO NOT:**

- Change logo colors or add effects
- Place logo on conflicting backgrounds
- Distort, rotate, or modify proportions
- Use low-resolution or pixelated versions
- Recreate or substitute fonts
- Animate logo without approval
