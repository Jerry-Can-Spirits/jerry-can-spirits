## 1. ~~Shopify storefront indexable~~ — FALSE POSITIVE, corrected. Real finding: Shopify canonical + redirect both point at headless 404s · HIGH · Shopify-side

**Correction (2026-07-20).** The original finding claimed the Shopify storefront
was indexable and would split ranking signal. That was wrong — it conflated
*crawlability* with *indexability*. The served HTML already carries an
unconditional noindex:

```
$ curl -s https://shop.jerrycanspirits.co.uk/products/hiball-glass-38cl
<meta name="robots" content="noindex">
<link rel="canonical" href="https://jerrycanspirits.co.uk/products/hiball-glass-38cl">
```

The Hydrogen redirect theme serves `noindex` on every page, so Google drops the
Shopify URLs from the index; there is no duplicate-content split. `robots.txt`
`Allow: /` is *correct alongside* the noindex, not in tension with it — a page must
be **crawlable** for Google to fetch it and *see* the `noindex`. Blocking it in
`robots.txt` would hide the noindex and risk the URL lingering in the index as a
"blocked" entry. So `Allow: /` + `noindex` is the right configuration. No change
needed to indexability.

**The real finding that same curl exposes — worse, and customer-facing.** Both the
canonical and the theme's redirect are built from `{{ request.path }}` **verbatim**,
so a Shopify path is passed straight through to the headless domain, whose route
structure does not match:

| | Shopify path | Passed through to | Result |
|---|---|---|---|
| Product | `/products/hiball-glass-38cl` | `jerrycanspirits.co.uk/products/hiball-glass-38cl` | **404** |
| Ours | — | `jerrycanspirits.co.uk/shop/product/hiball-glass-38cl/` | 200 |

Confirmed: `https://shop.jerrycanspirits.co.uk/products/hiball-glass-38cl` 404s
after the redirect. The same class of mismatch hits collections
(`/collections/*` → we serve `/shop/*`) and policies (`/policies/*` → renamed
routes). This breaks **real customers** arriving on Shopify URLs from old links, QR
codes and emails — not just crawlers — and the noindex does nothing to help them.

**Fix (Shopify theme settings, not this repo — values below):**
1. Populate the theme's `custom_redirects` setting so the redirect script rewrites
   the Shopify path prefix to the headless equivalent before redirecting (literal
   value in this doc / thread).
2. Correct the canonical, which `custom_redirects` does **not** touch — it is
   emitted directly from `{{ request.path }}` and will still point at a 404. Apply
   the same mapping in Liquid (recommended) so the canonical resolves; exact Liquid
   in this doc / thread.

The headless side already redirects `/pages/:slug → /:slug/` (`next.config.ts:342`)
and `/shop/drinks → /shop/spirits/`, but has **no** `/products/*` or `/collections/*`
handling, which is why those 404. Fixing it in the Shopify theme (per above) keeps
the mapping where the mismatch originates; the repo is not changed.

## 2. ~15 commercial collection pages have double-branded, truncated titles · HIGH · in-repo
`lib/categories.ts` `metaTitle` strings already end in `| Jerry Can Spirits®`,
and `layout.tsx:49-51` appends the template `%s | Jerry Can Spirits®` again.
`/shop/spiced-rum/` renders `British Spiced Rum | Small Batch | Jerry Can Spirits® | Jerry Can Spirits®`
(~73 chars, brand twice, truncated). Affects `[collection]/page.tsx:44` for the
~15 CATEGORIES-driven pages: `rum-gifts`, `spiced-rum`, `cocktail-making-kits`,
`bar-accessories`, `gifts-for-him`, `gifts-for-her`, `rum-glasses`, `hip-flasks`,
`ice-chilling`, `cocktail-glasses-glassware`, `cocktail-shakers`, `bundles`,
`new-releases`, `gift-sets`, `gifts-and-experience`. The hardcoded
`spirits/barware/clothing/drinks` pages are unaffected (their titles carry no
brand).

**Fix:** strip the trailing `| Jerry Can Spirits®` from every `metaTitle` in
`categories.ts` (template adds it once), or return `title: { absolute: … }`.

## 3. The three highest-value product titles render with a leading "- " · HIGH · in-repo (or Shopify)
`product/[handle]/page.tsx:141` strips `Jerry Can Spirits ` but leaves the ` - `
separator; live API confirms Expedition, the gift pack and the presentation box
have `seo.title: null` and a `"Jerry Can Spirits - X"` title, so the flagship
(96%-of-sales) `<title>` is `- Expedition Spiced Rum | Jerry Can Spirits®`.

**Fix:** `.replace(/^Jerry Can Spirits[®]?\s*[-–—]?\s*/i, '')`, or set `seo.title`
on those three products in Shopify. Products with `seo.title` set are unaffected
(the fallback isn't used for their title).

## 4. Educational content links to affiliates and other content — never to your own shop · HIGH · in-repo + founder
The Field Manual equipment/glassware guides (`field-manual/equipment/[slug]/page.tsx:261-290`
budget/premium affiliate links) and cocktail recipes (`CocktailRecipeDisplay.tsx:264`
glassware → equipment page; `313-320` ingredient → ingredient page) link *out* to
Amazon/Master of Malt and *sideways* to content, never to `/shop/barware/*` or the
PDPs. A whole high-margin category you stock gets **zero inbound internal links**
from its own content. The "Need the Rum?" CTA (`cocktails/[slug]/page.tsx:447-463`)
only renders for `baseSpirit === 'spiced-rum'`, so a multi-spirit cocktail *using*
Expedition gets no commercial link. Field Manual ingredient guides
(`ingredients/[slug]/page.tsx:333-345`) send "Buy Now" to Master of Malt with no JCS PDP link.

**Fix (needs a content→SKU mapping — founder to decide):** where a guide's subject
maps to a stocked SKU, add an internal CTA — e.g. "Shop our stainless steel
cocktail shaker" → `/shop/product/<handle>/`, "Browse rum glasses" →
`/shop/rum-glasses/`; link the house rum in ingredient rows to its PDP.

## 5. Product & ItemList JSON-LD flattens variant pricing and mismatches GTIN/availability · HIGH · in-repo
`product/[handle]/page.tsx:429-457` emits a single `Offer` with `price =
minVariantPrice` (the *Single* price) while `availability` (line 434) and `gtin`
(line 417) come from `variants[0]` (the *Pair*, ordered first) — a price/GTIN
mismatch Google Merchant can flag. `priceRange` only carries `minVariantPrice`
(type `shopify.ts:41-43`; query `352-357` never fetches `maxVariantPrice`). The
collection `ItemList` flattens the same way (`[collection]/page.tsx:116-124`).

**Fix:** fetch `maxVariantPrice`; emit `AggregateOffer` (`lowPrice`/`highPrice`/
`offerCount`/`priceCurrency`) when variants span prices, single `Offer` otherwise;
derive `availability` from `variants.some(v => v.availableForSale)`; do not emit a
single variant's GTIN alongside an aggregate price. Same for the ItemList.

## 6. `/shop/drinks/` is a thin, duplicate page cannibalising `/shop/spirits/` and `/shop/spiced-rum/` · MEDIUM · in-repo + founder
Live API confirms **no `drinks` collection exists**; `drinks/page.tsx:64` returns
`[]` and renders an empty-but-indexable page (`dynamic='force-dynamic'`, canonical
`/shop/drinks/`). Three routes target the same "British spiced rum" query over one
product (Expedition): `/shop/drinks/` ("British Spiced Rum"), `/shop/spirits/`
("British Craft Spirits"), `/shop/spiced-rum/` ("British Spiced Rum").

**Fix:** 308-redirect `/shop/drinks/` → `/shop/spirits/` in `next.config.ts` and
delete the dead route. Differentiating the spirits vs spiced-rum copy is a
separate **founder** copy decision.

## 7. `priceValidUntil` hardcoded `2026-12-31` overstates the £35 window · MEDIUM · in-repo
`product/[handle]/page.tsx:442`. The live price regenerates correctly via ISR
(`revalidate=3600`) + the products/update webhook `revalidatePath` (verified), but
the schema tells Google £35 is valid all year, when Expedition rises to £45 on
**1 Aug 2026**.

**Fix:** set `priceValidUntil` to `'2026-07-31'` for Expedition (a per-product
constant; others keep the year-end default), with a comment to update after the rise.

## 8. Residual molasses / Welsh-provenance claims contradict the producer removal — some inside FAQ JSON-LD · MEDIUM · founder decision
The producer-reference removal (#951/#952) was clean, but contradicting claims
survive: `sustainability/page.tsx:44` FAQ JSON-LD "source molasses locally where
possible"; `shop/spirits/page.tsx:401/458` FAQ "we use molasses";
`ingredients/expedition-spiced-rum/page.tsx:194/297` "Wales, UK / Pure Welsh Water".
These now contradict the deleted producer data and sit partly inside structured
data. Founder decision (copy + legal) — reported, not edited.

## 9. Trailing-slash inconsistency on internal product links → 308 hops sitewide · MEDIUM · in-repo
`trailingSlash: true`, and canonicals/sitemap honour it, but listing→PDP `<Link>`s
omit the slash: `[collection]/page.tsx:223`, `spirits:217`, `barware:195`,
`clothing:257`, related-products `product/[handle]/page.tsx:927`, and
`Breadcrumbs.tsx` (links and schema `item`). The cocktails page already includes
the slash (`cocktails/[slug]:341,424`). Every primary listing→product crawl hits a
308.

**Fix:** append `/` to these hrefs; note the convention in `CLAUDE.md`.

## 10. Breadcrumb depth inconsistency + non-slash JSON-LD URLs · MEDIUM/LOW · in-repo
Product breadcrumb starts at "Shop" (no "Home"), collection includes "Home"
(`product/[handle]/page.tsx:515-538` vs `[collection]/page.tsx:130-138`). Several
schema `url`/`item` fields omit the trailing slash so they don't byte-match the
canonical (`[collection]:113,123`, `Breadcrumbs.tsx:29`, `guides/[slug]:212`).

**Fix:** include Home on both; slash-terminate every schema URL.
